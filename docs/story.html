My Story

Hi, I'm Lakshya, a high school sophomore who is passionate about neuroscience, psychology, mental well-being, and research. I'm launching this blog to explore and advocate for how meditation and mindfulness can support adolescents, students, and anyone who is stressed, burned out, and overworked. As someone who tries to meditate regularly, I have noticed that even a few minutes of meditation can have a positive impact on my day. Meditation has helped me in numerous ways, such as reworking and training my negative thoughts into positive ones, becoming more focused, sleeping better, and much more. I believe that if more individuals had access to these tools, our generation could slow down and feel better, especially during the chaos of life! This blog will share: why meditation is important, especially for adolescents, the science-backed benefits of meditation for mental health and focus, a brief history of meditation and its cultural roots, an overview of different types of meditation anyone can explore, and my personal experiences.
